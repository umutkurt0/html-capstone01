🐾 Pip / Beast Companion (Unofficial)

🧾 Summary:

No one knows exactly what it is, but it's the joy of the group. Quietly observes, sometimes gives warnings, mostly sleeps. No one remembers how it arrived, but no one wants to give it up either.

Type: Unidentifiable tiny creature (probably magical)

Role: Moral support, bone burying, vocal night watch alerts

🛡️ Abilities:

  • Warm gaze and soft fur
  • Quiet observation
  • Occasional helpful warnings

🪓 Equipment:

  • None, but has warm looks and soft fur

🎲 Hobbies:

  • Getting into backpacks
  • Sleeping
  • Warming by the fire
  • Accidentally touching magical objects

Note: "Doesn't hit but conquers hearts."
